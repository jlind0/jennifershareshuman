Jennifer E. (Coleman) Quigley

Multi-disciplinary human with the willingness to transition between leadership styles to achieve the best results from a team. I have a passion for people with a strong operations background. I have experience in the public and private sectors. I was a top 5% sales performer in my role as a leasing consultant. I maintained a caseload 20% lower than agency average when I was a disability claims examiner. As an assistant manager, my store teams achieved top performance scores during internal and external audits for process execution. In both the Division Expense, Sanitation and Sustainability Manager and Safety Manager roles, I conducted audits in stores and coached store teams on corrective action items. I also rolled out company initiatives such as Lock out/Tag out, Slip and fall prevention and a cooking oil recycling program. I created relationships with vendors and educated store teams on contract obligations to improve quality of service to stores. I have experience with Food Safety, OSHA, DOT and FMCSA regulations. I have used and trained others on mobile technology in the retail, transportation and housing industries. I have facilitated presentations and training to people at all levels of an organization.

Many people have helped me throughout my life by giving me the opportunity to learn new skills, question the status quo and view obstacles to success from different perspectives. I believe each role you have is a building block for the next one and enables you to see your work with a wider lens.

I have discovered that my desire to connect with others in meaningful dialogue has created opportunities to bridge gaps in communication and reduce silos within organizations.

I want to see more women in executive level leadership roles and enjoy mentoring others to recognize their potential.

I believe leaders should foster an environment that empowers people to take initiative and be creative problem solvers to improve processes or experiences, even if they fail.

There should be open dialogue regarding successor planning with people at all levels of an organization on a frequent basis to keep teams motivated and encourage continued learning for personal and professional growth.

I care about the safety of others, reducing unnecessary expenses, efficiency and an equality between employee engagement and customer engagement.
